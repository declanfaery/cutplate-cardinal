Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

CutPlate Cardinal helps users build meal plans, recipe menus, grocery estimates, pantry-based recipe matches, and meal calendars.

Information We Collect

  • Name and email address for account setup and email confirmation.
  • Meal planning inputs, including preferred proteins, meal times, servings, allergies, goals, grocery budget, pantry ingredients, and optional shopping location such as ZIP code or city.
  • Generated meal plans, recipe selections, grocery estimates, and in-app calendar meals.
  • Basic technical information needed to run and secure the service.

How We Use Information

  • To create meal plans and recipe options based on user choices.
  • To estimate grocery needs and costs.
  • To send email confirmation and service-related messages.
  • To improve reliability, safety, and user experience.

Third-Party Services

CutPlate Cardinal may use service providers such as OpenAI for recipe generation, Render for backend hosting, Resend for email delivery, Expo for app builds, GitHub for code hosting, and Apple services for iOS distribution. These providers process information only as needed to provide their services.

Nutrition, Recipes, and Grocery Estimates

Recipe macros, calories, prices, and shopping lists are estimates for planning only. CutPlate Cardinal is not medical advice and does not diagnose, treat, or guarantee weight loss results. Users with medical conditions, allergies, or precise dietary needs should consult a qualified professional.

Sharing and Selling

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only with service providers, when required by law, or to protect the service and users.

Account Deletion

Users can delete their account from inside the app. Deletion removes the account information stored by the app backend. Some operational logs or provider records may remain for a limited period where required for security, legal, or service reliability reasons.
