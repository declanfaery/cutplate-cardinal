Support

Need help with CutPlate Cardinal? Email declan@palomasecurities.com.

Common Help Topics

  • Email confirmation: check spam or request a new confirmation from the app.
  • Recipe generation: free hosting can wake slowly after inactivity, so first requests may take longer.
  • Grocery prices: estimates vary by store, brand, season, and location.
  • Account deletion: open the app home screen and use Delete account.

Safety

CutPlate Cardinal provides planning estimates only. For allergies, medical conditions, or strict nutrition targets, consult a registered dietitian or medical professional.
